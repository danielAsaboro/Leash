---
title: "Mesh economy"
description: "Earn by serving inference, spend by borrowing it — settled on-chain per completion."
---

When the mesh is running, devices aren't just sharing compute for free — they're running
a micro-economy. A device that serves inference to peers **earns**; a device that
borrows inference from peers **spends**. The **Economy ledger** (/economy) shows the
balance sheet in real time, with per-transaction receipts settled on-chain.

The economy is **opt-in and disabled by default** (`HYPHA_ECONOMY_ENABLED=1` to enable).
The mesh works without it; pairing, delegation, and KV-cache reuse all function
regardless of whether settlement is on.

## Read the ledger

Go to **/economy**. Three headline figures refresh every 5 seconds:

| Figure | What it measures |
|---|---|
| **Earned (provider)** | Total received for serving delegated inference to peers |
| **Spent (consumer)** | Total paid for borrowing inference from peers |
| **Net** | Earned − Spent; green if positive, red if negative |

Below the figures: the settlement asset, your wallet address, and the network ID.

## Browse the market

The **Market** card lists every live peer that advertises a price. Each row shows the
peer's name, price per 1,000 tokens (µ-units of the settlement asset), reputation score,
and on-chain accountability status. The broker uses this table to route overflow turns —
cheaper peers with higher reputation scores win ties.

## View receipts

The **Receipts** card shows the most recent settlements. Each receipt has:
- Direction (earned / spent)
- Counterparty wallet (short address)
- Token count and amount
- On-chain transaction reference (links to the block explorer if
  `NEXT_PUBLIC_EXPLORER_BASE` is set)

Click **View all →** to open **/economy/receipts**, a paginated, filterable ledger
grouped by day. Filter by direction, counterparty, or date range.

## Enable and configure settlement

Set `HYPHA_ECONOMY_ENABLED=1` in the Hypha daemon's environment, then configure a
wallet and RPC endpoint for either the Plasma (EVM) or Solana rail. The demonstrated
path uses a local Anvil fork of Plasma (`eip155:9745`); for production, point the RPC
at a live node and fund the wallet.

All economy environment variables — wallet mnemonics, RPC URLs, asset mints, price per
k-token, spend caps, and forward-path metering — are listed in
[Configuration → Economy](/reference/configuration#environment-variables--economy-opt-in).
